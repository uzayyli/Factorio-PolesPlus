### Poles Plus

- Electric poles longer wire reach and supply area
- Electric poles have inbuilt lighting
- Underground pipes also have longer reach
- Yellow underground belts have 7 tiles reach (was 5 by default), now they have the same reach as Fast (red) underground belts
- All parameters are configurable (modify *before* startup)

- Default pole values compared to vanilla:

| Entity | Reach (V) | Reach (M) | Supply Area (V) | Supply Area (M) |
| ------------- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Small Electric Pole   | 7.5 | 16 | 5 | 8 |
| Medium Electric Pole  | 9 | 32 | 7 | 16 |
| Big Electric Pole  | 30 | 64 | 4 | 4 |
| Substation  | 18 | 64 | 18 | 32 |

- Other changes:

| Property | Vanilla | Mod (Default) |
| ------------- | :---: | :---: |
| Poles light radius  | - | 16 |
| Underground pipe reach  | 10 | 32 |
| Underground belt reach  | 5 | 7 |
| Roboport logistic range  | 25 | 32 |
| Roboport construction range  | 55 | 64 |

Extract the folder `PolesPlus_0.1.6` into `C:\Users\[you]\Roaming\Factorio\mods` (or `%APPDATA%\Roaming\Factorio\mods`) so you have the file `C:\Users\[you]\AppData\Roaming\Factorio\mods\PolesPlus_0.1.6\info.json`

Compatible with Factorio v2.0.7
