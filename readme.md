### Poles Plus

- Electric poles longer wire reach and supply area
- Electric poles have inbuilt lighting
- Underground pipes also have longer reach
- Underground belt (yellow) has 7 tiles reach, equal to Fast underground belt (red)
- Most parameters configurable

Extract the folder `PolesPlus_0.1.5` into `C:\Users\[username]\Roaming\Factorio\mods` (or `%APPDATA%\Roaming\Factorio\mods`)

Compatible with Factorio v2.0.7
